Community Bulletin

The ABC City Council is organizing several community events this month to encourage public participation and social engagement. Residents can enjoy cultural programs, food fairs, sports competitions, music festivals, and environmental awareness campaigns. These activities aim to bring together people of all age groups and strengthen community relationships. In addition, local volunteers will conduct cleanliness drives and tree plantation programs across various neighborhoods. The council encourages citizens to actively participate and contribute towards building a cleaner, greener, and more connected community. Registration for all events is available through the Community Event Portal.
